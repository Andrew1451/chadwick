ABOUT ME

Hi beauties! My name is Jessica. Allow me to tell you a little bit about myself. I have always had a passion for make up and art of all sorts. Now that I'm older, I am more invested in taking care of my skin than ever before. I am continually learning about make up and skincare, and I want to help others in the same way.

Back in high school, I struggled with stubborn under-the-skin acne which isn't easy to cover up when it's more than surface deep. A little after high school is when I was properly shown how to cleanse and care for my skin and that's when I started to notice a difference.

Why Mary Kay? I used it years ago and it improved my skin tremendously back when I wasn't as invested in my skin at the time. More recently, I love the culture and products, and decided I wanted to join that same enviornment. I'm learning how to turn my passion and love of art into my life's work. I will continue to educate myself in the world of skincare and makeup and hope to assist you as well no matter what.
